Who Owns The OK Furniture?

The mother company of the renowned OK Furniture is Shoprite Checkers (Pty) Ltd. Simply put, OK Furniture is the trading arm of Shoprite Checkers Ltd.

The Furniture company has over 300 stores and branches across many countries. Most of its branches are, however, located in African countries like South Africa, Zambia, Angola, Botswana, Swaziland, Namibia and Lesotho.

Paying for furniture at OK Furniture can be arranged with, or without deposit at affordable repayment terms. However, payment on most products must be done in 30 months.

History

OK Furniture was created as a stand-alone department in 1996 following the dimensionalisation of the various business segments bought by the South African Breweries in 1973.

The company started in a Johannesburg store – precisely at Pritchard Street- in 1927 and was known at the time as OK Bazaars by its founders, Michael Miller and Sam Cohen.

Shoprite Checkers bought the OK Group from the South African Breweries in 1997 after the OK Supermarkets market share was eroded in the 80’s and 90’s.
